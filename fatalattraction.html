Fatal Attraction

GitHub

Fatal Attraction is a fast paced, 3-player asymmetric video game set within a dating reality TV show called “Love in Paradise.” With an immersive 2D isometric view and retro 80s aesthetic, Fatal Attraction is a game about power and influence. If you play as the Producer, your goal is to maintain a positive, entertaining energy on the set. As the Admirer you should charm other contestants. Finally, as the Prophet you will attempt to convert as many contestants to your cause as possible. Overall, your goal is to increase NPC contestants’ affinity for you so you can influence them. But beware, you only have a single day (3-minute game time) to make a good impression!

Access the game directly on the GitHub repository, launch it on Godot, and play with two friends!
