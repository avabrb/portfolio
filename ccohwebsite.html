Overview

A central hub for all information about the Houston Consular Corps — upcoming events, photos from past gatherings, and member information. The goal is to build momentum for the Corps' work and make it easier for new consuls to join, while streamlining registration and the annual fee payment process through a secure member portal.

Key Features

Member Profiles & Directory
Secure profiles with contact info, payment history, and membership status. A searchable, filterable member directory for the entire Corps.
Events & Photo Feed
Google Calendar integration for upcoming events. A masonry photo feed for past gatherings, with admin-managed photo uploads and automatic date ordering.
Registration & Annual Payments
New members can register and pay annual fees directly through their profile. Admins track payment status and send reminders from a central dashboard.
Administrator Dashboard
Role-based access control for admins and members. Admins can approve registrations, manage renewals, upload club information, and monitor activity.
